Featured Games

Snake

Snake is a classic arcade game where players control a snake that moves across the screen, eating food items to grow longer. As the snake grows, it becomes more challenging to navigate without colliding with the walls or the snake's own body. The goal is to survive as long as possible while accumulating points by eating food.

Blackjack

Blackjack is a classic card game where players aim to beat the dealer by getting a hand value as close to 21 as possible without exceeding it. Players can choose to "hit" for additional cards or "stand" to keep their current hand. The game involves strategy, risk assessment, and a bit of luck.

Bird Blitz

Bird Blitz is a game where players attempt to dip, dive and dodge their way out of danger. The goal is dodge as many birds as possible!
